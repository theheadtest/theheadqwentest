Micro Ecosystem Simulator

Paint a tiny living world where water feeds grass, grass becomes forest, herbivores graze, predators hunt, and lightning turns dense habitats into temporary firebreaks. It is a compact cellular-automata sandbox for testing emergent balancing rules in one HTML file.
