🧪 Idea Lab

Welcome to our experimental playground! This is a space where we explore creative ideas through small, focused projects. Each experiment lives on its own page, free to grow and evolve as inspiration strikes.

🚀 Projects

🗺️Map Algorithm Comparison

Compare 5 different terrain generation algorithms side-by-side with the same seed. Includes Perlin Noise, Voronoi, Cellular Automata, Midpoint Displacement, and a Hybrid approach.

🪐Procedural Tiny Planets

Generate animated 2D impostor planets with per-pixel sphere projection, seeded 3D noise, fake lighting, atmosphere, clouds, craters, lava cracks, and multiple planet types.

🧊Voxel Location Generator

Generate a rotating isometric voxel diorama in five sequential stages: terrain skeleton, climate biomes, caves, decorations, and lighting polish.

🌿Micro Ecosystem Simulator

Paint a tiny living world where water feeds grass, forests spread, rabbits graze, foxes hunt, and fire reshapes the habitat through simple cellular automata rules.
